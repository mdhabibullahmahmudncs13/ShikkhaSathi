Hacking
Hacking is usually accessing or taking complete control of a website or comput-
er network without permission. The person who hacksis called the hacker.
Hacking can be legal or illegal. Some organizations hire hackers to test the secu-
rity of their systems. The work of these hired hackers is called legitimate hack-
ing. They check system security but do not harm the system.
For example, there are many legitimate hackers to
check the system. They are called white hat hackers.
Those who do illegal hacking are also called Crackers.
