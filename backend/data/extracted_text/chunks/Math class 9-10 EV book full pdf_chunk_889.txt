Reduction of fractions
Reduction of a fraction means to transfer the fraction to its lowest terms. For
this purpose, both the numerator and denominator are to be divided by their
common divisor or factor. If there is no common divisor or factor in between
numerator and denominator of a fraction, such fraction is said to be in its lowest
terms.
