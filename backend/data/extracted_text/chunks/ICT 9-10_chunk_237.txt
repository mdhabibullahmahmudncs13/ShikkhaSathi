72 Multimedia and Graphics
6. Interactive Computing: In this connection, we should know a piece of
information about interactivity. Application of any style, such as Microsoft
word scroll or flash, into the text or any effects of animation into the video
does not make the text or video interactive. Something created by the
powerful authoring software like ‘Flash®, ‘Director’, and “Authorware” that
makes interaction possible between the user and the computer is called
‘Interactivity’.
Use of Multimedia in TCT
There is an increased use of multimedia in ICT.
1. As teaching aid : The use of multimedia as a teaching aid is widely used.
The learner will understand a topic easily, if multimedia is used in the class
