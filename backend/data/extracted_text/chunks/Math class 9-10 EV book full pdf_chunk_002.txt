The importance of formal education is diversified. The prime goal of modern
education is not to impart knowledge only but to build a prosperous nation by
developing skilled human resources. At the same time, education is the best
means of developing a society free from superstitions and adheres to science

world of the 21st century, we need to ensure quality education. A well-planned

ethics. In this context, the government is determined to ensure education as per
the demand of the age.
