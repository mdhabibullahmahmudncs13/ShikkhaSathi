The Use of Stroke

The border of object is called Path. The measurement of making a path thick or

thin is called Stroke. Objects of grouped and ungrouped path, straight. curved

lines, ete. are controlled by Stroke. Controlling strokes means to make a path
thick or thin and colored. Making a Stroke thick and thin can be done with the

Stroke Palette. To get the job done

A. Select the object or path. If the Stroke Palette is not viewed on the screen,
click the Stroke command from the Window menu, and the Stroke Palette
will be viewed.
