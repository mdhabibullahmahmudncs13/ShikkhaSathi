- If any object is selected with the Selection Tool, rectangular, circular or
hounding box is created around the object. Four hollow boxes on four
corners and four on the four arms will be visible. These boxes are called
Resize Box. Clicking and dragging on the resizing boxes, the size of the
object can be enlarged and reduced. Keep pressing shift and drag, and the
object will be bigger or smaller according to proportion.

- Clicking the centre of the selected object with the Selection Tool or on any of
the arms or any part of the object and dragging, it will be shifted to anywhere.
