Merging Layers

After creating and editing objects of two or more layers, they can be merged

together. To get the job done:

A. Select the layers.

B. Select the ‘Merge Selected” command from the pop-up menu of the layer
palette, and the layers will be merged.

Use of Color in Object

After creating an object, fill in the color as required. The color palette, color bar,

or color spectrum bar, gradient, ete. are used to fill color in the object.

Color Palette

If the color palette is not viewed on the screen, give the color command of the

‘Window menu, and the color palette will appear.
