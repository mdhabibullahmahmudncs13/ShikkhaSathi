- Making squares, circles and objects is possible with the marquee tool within
the selected tool.

- By pressing the shift button and dragging Rectangular Marquee Tool, a
perfect square, and pressing the shift button and dragging Elliptical Marquee
Tool, a perfect circle can be drawn. I you drag while pressing the Alt button,
selections of square and circle can be extended from the centre point.

Shifting of Selection

- After making the square of selection by Rectangular Marquee Tool,
remove the finger from the mouse, and the selection of square will be
viewed on the screen.

- Ifyou take the mouse pointer inside the selection, a rectangular icon will be
viewed below the pointer. Pressing and dragging, the selection can be
shifted elsewhere.
