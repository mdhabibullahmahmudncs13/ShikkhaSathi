Different types of E-book

Nowadays, different kinds of e-book can be found. Different publication

requires different e-book format. Usually e-books are divided into the following

five categories:

* Exact copy of a printed version: These books are like their printed versions.
They are published in the pdf (portable document format) format. The
complete texts or parts are available on the internet.

+ E-books that are only readable on the internet: These books are usually
published in the HTML. This is called the website of books.
