There are some modes to view objects in the Illustrator programme. Any of these

can be selected from the View menu as required.

- In Outline Mode, the object is viewed as an outline or in a linear shape. In
this mode, editing of the shape or structure of the object, particularly a
detailed editing of a line or path can easily be done.

- The object is viewed perfectly in Pixel Preview or Preview mode.

- The image viewed in Overprint Preview Mode can be printed out.
