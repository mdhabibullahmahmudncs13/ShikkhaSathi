Removing Anchor Point

To remove any anchor point on the path:

A. Select the Delete Anchor Point Tool. After selecting the tool, bring mouse

pointer inside the screen, and a minus sign (-) will be viewed below the Pen Tool.

B. If you click any anchor point with the Delete Anchor Point Tool, that anchor

point will be deleted.
