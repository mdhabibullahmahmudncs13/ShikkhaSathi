Cancelling Layer

A. By selecting the Layer and the Delete command from the pop-up menu on
the layer palette, the selected layer will be cancelled.

- In the process of deleting a layer of artwork or an object. a cautionary
message like ‘Delete Layer?” will be viewed. Click the ‘Ok’ button. and the
layer will be deleted. Click the “No” button, and the deleting process will be
stopped. But by clicking and dragging the layer, on to the Delete icon, no
