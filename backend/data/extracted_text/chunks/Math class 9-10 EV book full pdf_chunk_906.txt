Types of Quadrilateral
Parallelogram:A parallelogram is a quadrilateral with
opposite sides parallel. The region bounded by a
parallelogram is also known as parallelogram. Parallclogram
Rectangle:A rectangle is a parallelogram with a
right angle. The region bounded by a rectangle is a
rectangular region.
Rectangle
Rhombus:A rhombus is a parallelogram with equal
adjacent sides, i.e., the opposite sides of a rhombus are
parallel and the lengths of four sides are equal. The
region bounded by a rhombus is also called rhombus. Rhombus
Square: A square is a rectangle with equal adjacent
sides, i.e., a square is a parallelogram with all sides
equal and all right angles. The area bounded by square
is also called a square. Square
