multimedia.
Multimedia is usually captured by a digital machine or device. It can be directly
presented on the stage or projected in a different way. Sometimes some
electronic devices are called multimedia because they contain the information
and directly display it in different forms. If we want to use three media in one
activity, we should start from the cinema. In the last phase of 19th century, in
1895, with the invention of cinema, text, graphics, sound and movement were
added to it. Therefore. cinema is called the earlier invention of multimedia. Even
though this form of multimedia came into being ages ago, the integration of the
computer to it is a quite a recent development.
